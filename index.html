RETRO DRAW

instructions


Pick Color with buttons on the left side
.
Click cell inside the grid to apply the color
.
Click again to erase the color
.
Click Fill All Cells to fill all of the grid
.
Click Fill All Empty Cells to fill all the empty cells
.
Click Clear All Cells to completely wipe the grid
.
Click Drag and Draw to activate the drag and draw feature
.
Click again to disactivate drag and draw feature
.
New Color Color gives you option to add a new color button of your choice
.
You have the option to type out a color name for example: salmon, #ff8c69, or rgb(255, 140, 105)
.
*if you do not know a color by name, hex, or rgb value you also have the option to select a color using the color selector above the New Color Color button
*
Confirm your selection by clicking on the New Color Color button
.
